Privacy Policy

IYKYK – If You Know, You Know

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how IYKYK (formerly Bro Code) handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

IYKYK does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • No account registration
  • No message content storage
  • No tracking across apps or websites
  • No advertising identifiers

2. Keyboard Data

IYKYK is a keyboard-based app. Any text you type using the keyboard remains on your device. We do not transmit or store your messages on our servers.

3. Analytics

IYKYK does not use third-party analytics or tracking tools that identify individual users.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate third-party services that collect personal data for tracking or advertising purposes.

5. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or stored, there is no risk of your personal information being exposed through our services.

6. Children’s Privacy

IYKYK does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
